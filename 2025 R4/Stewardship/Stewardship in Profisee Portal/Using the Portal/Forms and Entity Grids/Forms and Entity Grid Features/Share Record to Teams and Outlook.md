# Share Record to Teams and Outlook

The **Share to Teams/Outlook** feature allows you to share a record with other users in your organization and edit it in an external platform.

To share a record in Teams or Outlook:

1. Open the record you wish to share and click the vertical ellipsis icon.
2. ![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-id51803e89159662d.png)Click **Share to Outlook** or **Share to Teams**. This generates and copies an embed link to the record.
3. Paste the link into a Microsoft Teams message field or into the body of a Microsoft Outlook email field.

Once the record has been shared, you can choose to **Open in Portal** to work with the displayed data in Profisee, **Show Details**to view all attribute values given for the record, or **Edit**to make any changes to the data directly in Outlook or Teams. Once you are finished making any **Edit** changes, click **Submit** to publish the changes within Profisee.
