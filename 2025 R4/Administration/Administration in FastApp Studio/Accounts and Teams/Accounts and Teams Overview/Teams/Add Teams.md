# Add Teams

Add accounts to internal teams, and assign roles and permissions to those teams. After a team is added, you may use it in other feature areas. For example, you may assign Workflow tasks to the team through the Workflow feature area. For more information, see Adding sta tic workflow participants.

Add a new team:

1. In the navigation panel under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Teams**.
3. Click **New**.

The Teams edit panel opens.
4. On the **General** tab, add the following information:

- **Name**

The name used to identify the team
- **Description**

Optional descriptive text providing additional information about the team
- **Email address**

Optional email address for the team
5. On the **Accounts** tab, add accounts to the team:

1. Select the one or more accounts in the **Available accounts** pane.
2. Click the right arrow to add the accounts to the Assigned accounts pane.
6. On the **Roles** tab, [assign roles](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/assign_team_roles) to a team. All accounts in the team will be able to access the feature areas and perform the tasks allowed by these roles.

For more information, see [Roles Overview](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/roles_overview).
7. On the **Permissions** tab, assign permissions to the team. All accounts in the team will have the permissions assigned to the team.

For more information, see [Assigning Team Permissions](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/assign_team_permissions).
8. Click **Save and Close**.

Add accounts to Profisee from Active Directory to make them available for team membership.

Teams can only contain accounts. They cannot contain other teams.
