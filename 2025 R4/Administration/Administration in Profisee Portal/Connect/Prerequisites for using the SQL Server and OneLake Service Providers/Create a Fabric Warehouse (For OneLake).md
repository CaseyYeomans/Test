# Create a Fabric Warehouse (For OneLake)

You must create a Fabric warehouse to store and manage your data within OneLake. To create a Fabric Warehouse:

1. Log in to the Microsoft Fabric portal.
2. Navigate to your Fabric workspace.
3. Click the **+** button to add a new item.
4. Select **Warehouse** from the available options.
5. Provide a name for your warehouse.
6. Choose the desired settings and configurations.
7. Click **Create** to initialize your warehouse.

**Important Considerations**

- Ensure you have the necessary permissions in your Fabric workspace.
- Consider the warehouse size and scaling requirements for your use case.
- Review the data governance and security settings.

## Finish

You may now continue to the final step, [Enable App Registration Access](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/enable_app_registration_access).
