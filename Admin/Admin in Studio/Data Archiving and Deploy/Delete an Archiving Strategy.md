# Delete an Archiving Strategy

To delete a strategy:

1. In the navigation panel, under Administration, click **Data Archiving and Deployment**.
2. On the Data Archiving and Deployment tab, click **Archive data**.
3. On the Data Archiving tab, select the strategy.
4. On the strategy edit toolbar, click **Delete**.
5. Click **Yes** to confirm the deletion.

The strategy is removed from the list.

To delete multiple strategies:

1. Press **CTRL** or **SHIFT** and select multiple strategies.
2. On the strategy edit toolbar, click **Delete**.
3. Click **Yes** to confirm the deletions.

The selected strategies are removed from the list.
