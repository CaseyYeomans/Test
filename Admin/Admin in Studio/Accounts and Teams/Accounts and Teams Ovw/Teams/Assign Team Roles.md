# Assign Team Roles

Assign [Roles](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/roles_overview) to a team:

1. Under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Teams**.
3. In the list, select the team.
4. In the Teams toolbar, click **Edit**.

The Teams edit panel opens.
5. Click the **Roles** tab.
6. Select the roles to assign to the team in the **Available roles** column.
7. Assign the roles to the team by clicking the right arrow button.
8. Unassign any unneeded roles by selecting the roles in the **Assigned roles** column, and then clicking the left arrow button.
9. Click **Save and Close** when you have finished editing the team.
