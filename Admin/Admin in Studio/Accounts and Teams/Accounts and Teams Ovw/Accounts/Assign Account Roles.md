# Assign Account Roles

Assign roles to an account:

1. In the navigation panel under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Accounts**.
3. Select the account from the list of accounts.
4. In the Accounts toolbar, click **Edit**.

The Account edit panel opens.
5. Click the **Roles** tab.
6. Select the roles to assign to the account in the **Available roles** column.

When a role is selected, a detailed description displays at the bottom of the screen.
7. Assign the roles to the account by clicking the right arrow button.
8. Unassign any unneeded roles by selecting the roles in the **Assigned roles** column, and then clicking the left arrow button.
9. Click **Save and Close** when you have finished editing the account.
