# Assign Account Permissions

Assign permissions to an account:

1. In the navigation panel under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Accounts**.
3. Select the account from the list of accounts.
4. In the Accounts toolbar, click **Edit**.

The Account edit panel opens.
5. Click the **Permissions** tab.
6. Click **Edit**.

The **Edit Permissions** dialog appears.
7. Right-click the entities in the tree to make explicit permission assignments at the entity level.

The following permissions are available at the entity level:

- No Access
- Read
- Update
- Update Create
- Update Create Delete
- Administration

For more information, see [Permissions Overview](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/permissions_overview).
8. Expand the individual entity nodes to access the attributes, and then right-click the attributes to make explicit permission assignments at the attribute level.

The following permissions are available at the attribute level:

- No Access
- Read
- Update
9. Under the **Filters** node, edit the Read Filter and the Update Filter to enforce row-based security.

For more information, see [Understanding Filter-based Permissions](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/using_filter_permissions).
10. Click **Save** to save the permission assignments.
11. Review the account's explicit permissions on the Permissions tab to make sure the account has the expected rights to model data.
12. Click **Save and Close** when you have finished assigning permissions to the account.
