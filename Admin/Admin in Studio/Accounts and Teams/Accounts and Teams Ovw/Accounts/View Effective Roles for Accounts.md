# View Effective Roles for Accounts

To view effective roles for an account:

1. In the navigation panel, Under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Accounts**.
3. Select an account in the list of accounts.
4. In the Accounts toolbar, click **View**.
5. Click **Effective Roles** on the menu.

The Effective Roles dialog appears.

A list of roles displays for the account. The roles listed are those that are explicitly assigned to the account plus those which are inherited through team assignments.
