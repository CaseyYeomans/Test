# Float and Dock Workspace View Tabs

## Float a Workspace View Tab

You can open any workspace tab in a single floating window. Use floating tabs to tile or cascade multiple panes in one monitor, or expand a single floating tab to fill the entire screen of a secondary monitor.

- Right-click any view tab in your workspace and then click **Float** on the menu.

## Dock a Workspace View Tab

- In the upper left corner of the floating tab, click **Dock**.
