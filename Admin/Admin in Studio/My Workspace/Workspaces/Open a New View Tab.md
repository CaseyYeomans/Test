# Open a New View Tab

1. From anywhere in your workspace, click New Tab.

**--or--**

Expand **My Workspace** in the navigation pane, if necessary, and then click **New**.

**--or--**

Right-click a tab already open in your workspace, and then click **New** on the menu.
2. Select a view type for the new tab.
3. Make the necessary selections for the new tab, and then click **OK**.

The new tab opens with your selections.
