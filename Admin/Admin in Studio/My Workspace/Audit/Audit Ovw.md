# Audit Overview

You can review data change details in an entity, including a history of what was changed, when, and by whom, with Audit. Create and maintain a historical record of data in log form. Review your data over time to track trends and build projections to support future efforts.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-ia83150e3e64730eb.png)

View data change history at the member level with Audit:

1. In the **My Workspace** tab, selected the desired entity grid.
2. Select the desired member record.
3. Right-click and select **View Details** from the dropdown menu. Data Quality and History panes open at the bottom of the page.
4. View History details in the right-hand pane. Click an entry to expand the details panel and view a list of attributes associated with the member. Changes appear in this list.
