# Edit a Portal Application Page

1. In the navigation panel, under Administration, click **FastApps**.
2. Access the Pages tab.
3. On the Pages, tab, select the page that you want to edit.
4. Click **Edit** in the Pages tab toolbar.

The information in the Pages tab grid is read-only until you click **Edit**.

These default pages are not editable: Analytics, Audit, Notifications

These default pages are editable: Home, Work

All user-created pages are editable.
5. You can make the following changes:

- Add a control to an empty zone by clicking **Add** in the zone and selecting the control to add.
- Delete an existing control from a zone by right-clicking the control and then clicking **Delete** on the menu.
- Edit the properties of an existing control by selecting the control and then updating the **Properties** options.
- Drag a control to a new zone by clicking the control and dragging it to the new location.
- Copy a control to a zone by selecting the control, pressing CTRL, and dragging the control to a zone.
6. Click **Save** to save your changes.
7. Click **Back** to change the identifying information about the page:

- In the **Name** field, enter a name for the page.
- In the **Description** field, enter text describing the purpose of the page (optional).
- In the **Layout** section, select the zone to contain the controls on the page.
8. Click **Save**, or, if you want to edit the page zone information, click **Next**.

If you have selected a new zone layout with fewer zones than the original layout, the additional zones will be removed from the page. A warning message displays to let you know that this will happen.

If you select a new zone with more zones than your original layout, then any controls will shift up in sequence, filling the first zones in the page, and leaving the empty zones at the end. You can rearrange the zones as you prefer.

9. Click **Save** to save your changes.
