# Configure Applications Based on Team Association

You can filter applications according to team, so team members will only see apps they need to access.

1. In FastApps Studio, select **FastApps**.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-i98327ebe6f2c3ee9.png)
2. Select the app you want to configure. Double-click to open the administration panel.
3. Click the **Teams** tab.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-ic47ecedf7f59498d.png)
4. Cick **Edit**.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-i75fb2296075f3ca9.png)
5. Click the **Selected Teams** radio button.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-if9c9c8852f4d7eb8.png)
6. Use the arrow button between the two panes to move a team from Available Teams to Assigned Teams. Once the team is assigned, the app will only be available to members of that team.

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-if1e9117d540b3bdb.png)
