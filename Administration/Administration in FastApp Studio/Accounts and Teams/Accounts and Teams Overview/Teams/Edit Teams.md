# Edit Teams

Edit a team:

1. Under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Teams**.
3. In the list, select the team you want to edit. The Teams edit panel opens.
4. On the General tab, edit any of the following information:

- **Display name**

The name used to identify the team
- **Description**

Optional descriptive text providing additional information about the team
- **Email address**

Optional email address for the team
5. On the Accounts tab, add accounts to the team:

1. Select the one or more accounts in the **Available accounts** list.
2. Click the right arrow to add the accounts to the **Assigned accounts** list.
6. On the **Roles** tab, [assign roles](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/assign_team_roles) to a team. All accounts in the team will be able to perform the tasks allowed by these roles.

Each account in the team is assigned the roles at are assigned to the team. For more information, see Understanding roles.
7. On the **Permissions** tab, assign permissions to the team. All accounts in the team will have the permissions assigned to the team.

Each account in the team is assigned the permissions that are assigned to the team. For more information, see Assigning team permissions.
8. Click **Save and Close** when you have finished editing the team.

"Available account description" displays the description for the selected account in the Available accounts list. "Assigned account description" displays the description for the selected account in the Assigned accounts list.
