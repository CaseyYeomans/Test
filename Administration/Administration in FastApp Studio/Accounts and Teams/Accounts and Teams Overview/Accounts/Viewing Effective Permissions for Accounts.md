# Viewing Effective Permissions for Accounts

To view [Effective Permissions](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/effective_permissions) for an account:

1. In the navigation panel, Under Administration, click **Accounts and Teams**.
2. Click **Accounts**.
3. Select an account in the list of accounts.
4. In the Accounts toolbar, click **View**.
5. Click **Effective Permissions** in the menu.

The Effective Permissions dialog appears.

The selected account has some type of permission to any objects appearing in bold in the dialog, either implied or explicit. The permission appearing to the right of the model object is the account's effective permission to that model object.
