# Close Workspace View Tabs

## Close a Single Tab

- Move the pointer over the workspace view tab and click the **X** on the right side.
- **--or--**
- Right-click the workspace view tab and then select **Close** on the menu.

## Close all Workspace Tabs

- Right-click any workspace view tab and then select **Close All** on the menu.
- **--or--**
- Click **Close the current workspace** on the main toolbar.
