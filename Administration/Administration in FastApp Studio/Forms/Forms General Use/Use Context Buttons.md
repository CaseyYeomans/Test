# Use Context Buttons

The Form Properties dialog includes the option to configure form context buttons. These are the default buttons that appear on forms that are associated with particular types of activities configured in a workflow, or through the entity grid control in a FastApps Portal application page:

**Approve Task**

- Applies to forms involving Approve activities
- Configures the Approve and Reject buttons to the bottom of the form

**Contribute Task**

- Applies to forms involving the Contribute Task
- Configures the **Save** and **Save and Continue** buttons at the bottom of the form

**Add Form**

- Applies to the **Save**button appearing on the Add dialog launched from the entity grid control in FastApps Portal
- Configures the **Save** and **Save and Close** buttons

**Update Form**

- Applies to the **Save** button at the bottom of the Edit dialog launched from the entity grid control in FastApps Portal
- Configures the **Save** and **Save and Close** buttons
