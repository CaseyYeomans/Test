# Global Sidebar

The Global Sidebar appears consistently throughout the Portal and allows users to configure settings, view notifications, access help and support, and 9when applicable) access Administration view in Profisee Portal.
![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-iaaf1f5101098cc15.png)

By clicking the vertical ellipss icon in the righthand corner, users can open the **Settings** menu. From this menu, a user can:

- **Help:**Access the online help
- **Support:** Contact Profisee customer support
- **About:**Open a new window containing information about this instance of Profisee
- **View:** Toggle items on the page between open view and compact view.

By clicking the Bell icon, you can view your ten most recent notifications, or click **View more...** to access a full [notification list](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/viewing_notifications).

![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-ib625be64bff8ad4f.png)

By clicking your user name or icon, you can log out of the portal.![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-ic6b1d867282d7432.png)The Sidebar allows you to quickly access important functions in the portal, including:

- **Administration**: Access the Administration area of the portal.
- **FastApps**: Opens a list of FastApps accessible by the user.
- **Data Browser**: Access data from the FastApp Portal homepage without needing to navigate through individual FastApps and entities.
- **Refresh Portal**: Refresh the portal cache to see recent updates.
