# FastApps Portal App Tray

The App Tray is a collapsible panel that opens to show all the apps you have access to. It is available from any app page in Portal via the global sidebar.

Select the FastApps bar on the bottom left corner of the screen, either from the collapsed or expanded view.
![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-ib97dd0d2dc6ed0a1.png)

The app tray displays all applications you have access to, with the app you're currently working in highlighted
