# View Matching Results in FastApps Portal

Opening a Match Group to the Split/Combine view displays the Match Stewardship window. ![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-i4360c15c90c4fb72.png)

Here, all records in the Match Cluster are displayed with the Golden Record at the top. The match attributes for each record as displayed as columns within the table. This table also displays the Match Status of each match record, as well as the Model Confidence in the matching result. From here, you can perform a [Fuzzy Match or Filtered Search](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/fuzzy_match_and_filter_search) to easily locate one or several records within the Match Cluster.

The lefthand Match Clusters field allows you to [Split or Combine](https://support.profisee.com/wikis/profiseeplatform/splitcombine_members) records into one or more Match Clusters to better organize your data.
