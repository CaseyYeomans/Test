# View Record Quality in a FastApps Portal Form

You can view the data quality status of recordss within the Profisee FastApps portal by using the View Data Quality tab. This view lets you identify data quality issues of attributes within an individual record.

To view data quality in the Profisee web portal:

1. Click a record you want to review, or right-click the record and select **Open**.
2. Navigate to the **View Data Quality** tab.![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/staticasset/read/file-icdc37121f2d3410a.png)

You can view quality details in the Data Quality window. Each attribute for which there is a relevant data quality rule appears in this window. Data quality issues are highlighted by the presence of one of the following icons to the left of the input field on the form:

- The exclamation mark icon indicates that the field has a data quality issue. You can hover over the icon to retrieve details on the data quality error. ![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-i48c458a4935981dc.png)
- The green checkmark icon indicates the field has no data quality issues. ![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-i9e59a96d4ad164b9.png)![](https://Profisee.magentrixcloud.com/sys/StaticAsset/Read/file-i3694d721da1b4757.png)
